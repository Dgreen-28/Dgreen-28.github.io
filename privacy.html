Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Studio DG Apps builds iOS apps designed to be useful, simple, and respectful of user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our apps may collect, use, and protect information.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app and features used, our apps may collect limited information such as app usage data, crash diagnostics, purchase status, and advertising-related signals. This information helps keep the app working, improve performance, and support optional monetization features such as ads or in-app purchases.

Advertising

Some Studio DG Apps may show ads using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may use device identifiers, approximate location, app activity, and other signals to serve, measure, and improve ads, depending on user consent and device settings.

In-App Purchases

Some apps may offer in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store full payment card details.

Analytics and Diagnostics

We may use basic analytics or diagnostic information to understand app performance, identify crashes, and improve the user experience. This information is generally aggregated or technical in nature.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and address it.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to operate, improve, support, or comply with legal obligations related to our apps.

Your Choices

You can control many privacy choices through iOS settings, including app permissions, tracking permissions, notification settings, and advertising preferences.
