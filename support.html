Support

Need help with one of our apps?

We want every Studio DG Apps experience to feel smooth, clear, and reliable. Reach out if you have a question, bug report, purchase issue, or feature suggestion.

Contact

Say What Now?

For gameplay, hints, ads, purchases, Daily Puzzle streaks, or Game Center questions, send a support email with “Say What Now?” in the subject line.

Purchases

Purchases are handled through Apple. If something does not unlock correctly, contact support and include the approximate purchase date.
